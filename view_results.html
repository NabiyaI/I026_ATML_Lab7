💡 Key Insights

🧠
Information Bottleneck Solved
Without attention, all source info is compressed into one fixed vector. Attention lets the decoder dynamically attend to any encoder output at each time step.
📐
Alignment & Interpretability
Attention weights visualize source-target word alignment, making the model's translation decisions interpretable and linguistically meaningful.
📏
Variable-Length Handling
Attention prevents gradient vanishing on long sequences by giving the decoder direct access to all encoder outputs, not just the final state.
⚖️
Complexity vs. Generalization
On small datasets, the simpler model generalizes better. The larger attention model benefits significantly from more data and longer sequence lengths.
